About Me

Hi, I am N Kumar— pursuing a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (Control & Automation) at Texas A&M University, specializing in autonomous systems, robotics, and intelligent control. I have extensive experience in motion planning, UAV scheduling, SLAM, and adaptive control, with proven real-time deployments on both drones and ground robots.


My research has been published in IEEE Robotics & Automation Letters and AIAA SciTech, with ongoing work under review at IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems. I am passionate about developing robust and scalable robotic systems for autonomy in challenging real-world environments.
