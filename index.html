Research interest

I am a Master's student at Texas A&M University. My research interest lies in robotics, optimization, control and automation. My current research focuses on the persistent scheduling of battery-limited drones on charging pads to achieve long-term autonomy. I have developed a novel algorithm based on mixed-integer linear programming to address this problem. Below are the simulation results for four drones tasked with periodically monitoring 100 sites and returning to the starting depot to recharge. Our algorithm determines which sites each drone should visit based on their battery capacity and schedules them to minimize the number of charging pads required at the starting depot.
