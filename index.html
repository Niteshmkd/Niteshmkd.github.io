About Me

Hi, I am Nitesh Kumar—a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering (Control & Automation) at Texas A&M University, specializing in autonomous systems, robotics, and intelligent control. I have extensive experience in motion planning, UAV scheduling, SLAM, and adaptive control, with proven real-time deployments on both drones and ground robots. My research has been published in IEEE Robotics & Automation Letters and AIAA SciTech, with ongoing work under review at IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems. I am passionate about developing robust and scalable robotic systems for autonomy in challenging real-world environments.

Skills: Python, C++, ROS, Julia, MATLAB, Robotics, Kalman Filter, Reinforcement Learning, Adaptive Control
